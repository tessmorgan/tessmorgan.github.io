History of composting in Seattle

year act
1988 Seattle prohibited yard waste from the garbage
2005 Seattle prohibited recyclables from the garbage
2005 Seattle began curbside food waste collection
2009 Seattle required all residential properties to either subscribe to food and yard waste collection or participate in backyard composting
2011 Required multi-family buildings required to provide compost collection service to residents
2015 Seattle prohibited food waste from the garbage
