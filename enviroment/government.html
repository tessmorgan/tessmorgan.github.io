Importance of Power that a State's Government Holds

A state's government possesses a great amount of power that can be put to good use. They are able to create policies on commerce, taxation, healthcare, education, and many other issues within their state. There is a wide range of possibilities of how the government can put these specific means to use (including helping the envionrment); and it is important that they make use of this power and do the most with it because if they don't there would be no change.

Reducing Emissions

Gov. Inslee signed several policies ensuring that Washington state is doing its part to reduce emissions and restore the envionrment. As of November 2020, Washington's clean energy industry employed over 85,000 workers and accounts for more than 25% of all construction jobs in the state.

Funding Land Conservation Efforts

The Washington state Government funds a program called "the Washington Wildlife and Recreation Program". The goal of this program is to protect the enviornment in two main ways: acquiring valuable recreation and habitat lands before they are lost and developing recreation areas for a growing population. The government funding is used towards a broad range of land conservation efforts, inclduing protection of the natural areas near big cities to the protection of very rare, last remaining plant species in the world.
