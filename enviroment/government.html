Importance of Power that a State's Government Holds

A state's government possesses a great amount of power that can be put to good use. They are able to create policies on commerce, taxation, healthcare, education, and many other issues within their state. There is a wide range of possibilities of how the government can put these specific means to use (including helping the envionrment); and it is important that they make use of this power and do the most with it because if they don't there would be no change.
