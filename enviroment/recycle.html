Facts and Figures

here is some unique info on Seattle recycling

  1. 28% is recycled in Seattle, 38% is recycled in the Northwest and North America, and 34% is recycled in Asia

  2. Every year Americans throw away enough office paper to build a 12-foot wall from Seattle to New York

  3. It takes as few as 30 days for a glass container to go from a recycling bin to being back on the shelf
