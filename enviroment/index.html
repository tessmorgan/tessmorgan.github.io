Introduction

Washington is an amazing state full of beautiful scenery from mountain ranges, and bodies of water, forest, beaches, and much more. Our state values to keep our air and water clean and reducing waste so future generations will continue to enjoy a healthy, safe, and clean city. Unfortunately, do to our rise in population over time our impacts to the environment rise as well. Heavy city traffic emits carbon emission into our air and bodies of water have been suffering do to our waste. This is a call to action urgently requesting upon the residents of Seattle to learn about the beautiful state we live in, acknowledge our impacts, and gain insight into how to reduce and fight back against the war to save our state.

Importance

Humans depend on our Earth to survive. It is a necessity, not something we just have. This is the only home humans have and that's why it is essential to bring up environmental issues. Often, humans undermine how much we rely on our Earth to survive. Nowadays when you think of where your food comes from you think of the grocery store, you feel air blowing on your face while you're lounging on the coach from your air conditioning, and your water is dispensed from your refrigerator. We forgot where our necessities needed for survival actual come from. Our own actions are coming with consequences. Fate of humanity is determined by how we address these issues and our next moves as a society.

What is Enviromental Justice?

It's a term and movement to fight against the reality that vulnerable and marginalized communities are far more likely to experience pollution and contamination at a disproportionate level. People of color, poor people, and others are more likely to live near hazardous waste facilities, have lead contamination in the water, mold in public and private buildings, and so much more
