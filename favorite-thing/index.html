My Favorite Thing About School:

Girls' Empowerment Club!

Girls' Empowerment Club is a club open to all who identify as female in the EPS Community in hopes to cultivate a safe space for gender-based equity discussions, create a supportive community, and empower each other socially and academically. Often we address current world issues regarding gender, subjects around the idea of feminism, and ways to involve yourself while supporting everyone up in aspirations of a more inclusive world. You can find us during clubs on Thursdays in Tali 311 having a passionate discussion, educating ourselves, doing self-care or other activities that apply to our club moto of empowerment. We hope to see you there!

GEC's Impact on me:

Girls' Empowerment Club is one of my favorite things about school because it is a fun and supportive safe space where a great group of girls gets the chance to talk about anything and everything from understanding feminism better to disscussing current issues that arise. I joined the club in my freshman year and became a Co-Leader with Rose in my junior year. It has been a blast running the club and sharing our mission of empowering eachother. I can't wait to see what this club's year has in store for us!
