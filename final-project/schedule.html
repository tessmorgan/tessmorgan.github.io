Recent Shows

Earthlight Gems & Minerals recently went to the 41ST Show Of Gems, Minerals, & Fossiles presented by Portland Regional Gem & Mineral Show. This event lasted from Septmeber 30th to Octuber 2nd of 2022 in the Expo Hall-West in the Wingspan Event & Conference Center.
The show included many dealers with fossils, rocks, gems, minerals, beads, tools, and jewelry. There were many exhibits of fossils, lapidary arts, minerals, and faceted gems.
Additionally, there were demonstrations on the process of creating precious stones, wire-wrapping, and more, plus presentations, Door and Raffle prizes, and Silent Auctions.

The show benefits 5 non-profit clubs who support sharing the knowledge, and interest in geology, Lapidary arts, and related subjects.

If you want to see us in the future note that admission was $7 for the weekend pass, kids 12 and under were free with a paying adult. Parking was $10 per vehicle
